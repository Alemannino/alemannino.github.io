Profile

Welcome family, friends, visitors, and potential employers! My name is Alessandro Mannino. I am a 2014 Coastal Carolina University graduate who majored Business Management with concentration in Entrepreneurship.

Born in Palermo, Italy, I moved to the United States when I turned 7. I grew up in a small town in northern New Jersey, 20 minutes outside New York City. Since High School I have been involved in sports and extracurricular activities.

In college I remained active, being part of various organizations and holding multiple executive board positions. I also completed two fantastic internships with OmegaFi and Cowork MYR.

My dream has always been to build awesome products, "the next big thing". In college I interned at an incubator and helped a few founders take their ideas to the MVP stage. I have also attended both Startup Weekend and Lean Startup Machine placing 1st and 3rd respectively. My exceptional management, research and analytical skills have helped many founders bring their ideas to life. I'm a passionate product specialist looking to evolve into a Product Manager.

Friends and colleagues describe me as self-driven, motivated, dependable, and creative (particularly tactically). My areas of expertise include ideation, user research, rapid prototyping and business development.
